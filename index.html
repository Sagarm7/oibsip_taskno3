Biography of Cristiano Ronaldo :

  • Cristiano Ronaldo, full name Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal.
  • Ronaldo began his professional football career at Sporting Lisbon in Portugal before moving to Manchester United in 2003.
  • During his time at Manchester United, Ronaldo won three English Premier League titles, one FA Cup, two League Cups, and the UEFA Champions League in the 2007-2008 season.

  • In 2018, Ronaldo moved to Juventus, an Italian club, in another high-profile transfer. He played for Juventus for three seasons, winning two Serie A titles during his tenure.
  • Ronaldo has also achieved great success on the international stage. He has represented the Portuguese national team since 2003 and has been an integral part of their success.
  • Ronaldo helped Portugal win the UEFA European Championship in 2016 and the UEFA Nations League in 2019. He is the all-time leading scorer for the Portuguese national team.
  • Ronaldo has won the FIFA Ballon d'Or award five times in his career, recognizing him as the best football player in the world.
  • In August 2021, Ronaldo returned to Manchester United, signing a two-year contract with the club. His return to Manchester United was met with great excitement from fans around the world.
